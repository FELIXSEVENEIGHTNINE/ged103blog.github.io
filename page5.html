Rizal Park and Shrine

Dapitan City is the land where the Philippines National Hero, Dr. Jose P. Rizal, spent his four years of exile. Dapitan City is called “The Shrine City of the Philippines” and is located in the Zamboanga del Norte region. The city is rich in historical treasures and natural attractions.

This landmark in Dapitan, Zamboanga City, sits in the estate bought by Jose Rizal himself using his lottery winnings. This Shrine is built in honor of Dr. Jose Rizal who was exiled for the patriotic deeds fought for the country.

Visiting the Shrine is free of charge, but you can help the park administrators by maintaining the place’s cleanliness. Plain and simple… just put your trash in the designated garbage bin.
